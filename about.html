About

HI, I'M STANLEY

I am a 4th year at the University of Southern California studying Computer Science/Business Administration. I have been programming for just over 2 years now, and in that time I've found that I'm incredibly passionate about creating and innovating through the development of efficient software technologies.

PROGRAMMING

I love to learn. I am excited about the limitless potential of opportunity the advancement of techonlogy provides. I believe the best way to learn is to collaborate with others who come from different backgrounds and experience levels. Thus, I enjoy participating in Hackathons, attending workshops, and taking on challenging, new projects with friends!

INVOLVEMENT

When I am not programming, I enjoy spending my time becoming more involved with my community. At USC, I am a brother and Website Chair of the Kappa Sigma Fraternity's Delta Eta chapter, and a member of ACM2 --the Association of Computing Machinery's premier leadership program.

BSA

I was a boy scout of Troop 93 based in Fullerton, CA for 8 years. As a member of the BSA, I was given plentiful opportunites to develop leadership and communication skills, as well as outdoor camping skills. I earned my Eagle Scout rank on the 20th of November in 2015.

INTERESTS

Some of my hobbies include traveling, attending concerts with friends, and in the wintertime, snowboarding!
